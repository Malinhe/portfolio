About me

Hi, I am a Front-end developer based in Bremerhaven, Germany. I take pleasure in solving problems and bringing complex ideas to life. My ambition is to continue my career as a developer and find a position as a junior web developer.

I started my IT journey at a 4-week workshop for women. During this workshop, I was able to explore different areas of IT. I enjoyed programming the most and found it to be the best fit for my skills.

Standing still is not for me, I constantly need fresh input. That's why I love my job as a programmer, because constant further training is required here due to new technologies. Analytical thinking and solution-oriented action are what makes me special. My guiding principle is: There is a solution for every problem.
